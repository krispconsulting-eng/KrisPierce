## Shaping questions

- What's your father's defining characteristic, shown in one story?
- What did you inherit from him — the traits you're glad of and the ones you fight?
- How did he handle Will's diagnosis? Men of his generation often had no language for it — what did he DO instead of say?
- What was it like to watch him care for Mum? What did his caregiving teach you about your own?
- What's your relationship with him now — and what's changed since Mum?
- What conversation with Dad has never happened that should? (Whether or not it goes in the book.)
- Is he alive as you write? What are his review rights, and what would hurt him that doesn't need to be on the page?
