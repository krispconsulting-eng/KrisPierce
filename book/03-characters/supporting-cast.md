# Supporting cast

Rule: a supporting character earns a name and a face only if they change
something. Everyone else is texture. Composite characters are acceptable IF
the book discloses it (standard memoir practice) — decide in
`../01-vision/vision.md` ethics section.

For each person who makes the cut: name (real/changed/role-only) ·
one-line biography · what they changed · their key scene.

## Doctors & clinicians

- Who delivered the diagnosis, and how — cruelly, kindly, clumsily? (The
  manner of telling is a scene in itself.)
- Who was the doctor who got it RIGHT — the one you'd clone if you could?
  What exactly did they do differently?
- Who dismissed you? What did being disbelieved as a mother-who-was-also-a-nurse feel like?
- Which clinician relationship changed over years — adversary to ally, or the reverse?

## Researchers

- Who was the first researcher who made you feel the science might move in Will's lifetime?
- What's it like handing your child's genome, history, hope to a lab?
- Any researcher who became a friend? Any who disappointed?

## Friends

- Who stayed? Name the friend who showed up in year one AND year ten — what do they actually do that others don't?
- Who drifted — and do you understand why now, even if it hurt then?
- What's the truest thing a friend ever said to you? The worst well-meant thing?
- Who is the friend you can be UN-strong with?

## Other families

- The first SCN2A family you ever spoke to: what did that first contact feel like — recognition, terror, relief?
- Which family is a few steps ahead of you on the road, and what did watching them teach you? Who's behind you, watching *you*?
- Families you've lost — children in the community who died. How does the book hold that grief honestly without borrowing it?
- Rivalries or fractures in the community — advocacy isn't all solidarity. How honest will the book be about that?

## Carers, teachers, support workers

- Which paid carer became family? Which school or teacher changed Will's trajectory?
- What does it feel like to depend on a rotating cast of strangers in your home?

## The officials

- MPs, bureaucrats, funders: who listened? Who performed listening? What's the difference, from the chair you sat in?
