# Will

## Rule one

**Never reduce him to illness.** Will is a whole person; SCN2A is context.
Every scene featuring Will must pass the test: *would this scene still be
interesting if the reader forgot the diagnosis?*

## Arc

Baby → Diagnosis → Growing child → Family centre → **Young man**

## Biography

- Born when, where, after what journey?
- What was he like as a baby — before and apart from any of it?
- Milestones in HIS terms, not the clinic's: firsts, discoveries, jokes,
  obsessions, triumphs.

## Shaping questions

### Who Will is
- What makes Will laugh? What's his sense of humour actually like — give three real examples.
- What does Will love? (Music, water, food, people, routines, places — the specifics.)
- What is Will *good* at? What does he do better than anyone else in the family?
- How does Will communicate — and what have you learned to hear that strangers can't?
- What would Will's own chapter say, if he narrated one? (Consider actually writing this — a chapter in his sensory world.)
- What are his dislikes, moods, mischief? Whole person means flaws and stubbornness too.

### Will and the family
- "Family centre" — how did the family come to orbit him, and what does that gravity feel like from inside? When is it warm and when is it heavy?
- What has Will taught each member of the family that nothing else could have?
- Describe a moment of pure, uncomplicated joy with Will — no shadow, no lesson. The book needs several of these.

### The diagnosis and after
- The first signs: what did you notice, and what did the nurse in you know before the mother in you could accept?
- Diagnosis day: the room, who spoke, the exact words if you remember them, what you did in the car park after.
- What did you Google that night?

### The young man
- Who is Will now? What does his adulthood look like, and what did you have to fight for to get it?
- The question every carer-parent carries: *what happens when I'm gone?* Where does that live in the book?

### Ethics (feeds `01-vision/vision.md` boundaries)
- What would Will not want on the page, as best you can know him?
- Who checks Will's portrayal besides you?
