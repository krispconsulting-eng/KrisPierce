# Scene Library

Probably the biggest folder in the project. **Target: 150–250 scenes.**
Every memory, every conversation, every turning point, every emotional
moment gets captured here — rough, fast, unpolished. Scenes are raw
material; chapters are built from them later.

## How to capture a scene

One file per scene: `NNN-short-slug.md` (e.g. `014-diagnosis-day.md`).
Copy `_scene-template.md`. **Ten rough lines now beat a polished page
never.** Capture in any order — the inventory below imposes order later.

A scene is: a specific time, a specific place, specific people, something
happens. "The IVF years were hard" is not a scene. "The morning the second
cycle failed and I went to work anyway and hung a bag of someone else's IV
fluids" is a scene.

## Shaping questions for mining more scenes
- What smell instantly transports you — and to where?
- What object in your house holds the most story? (The book can tour objects: a syringe driver, a recipe card, a conference lanyard.)
- What conversation do you replay when you can't sleep?
- What moment would you save in a fire, if memories were objects?
- What's the funniest thing that ever happened in the middle of the worst time? (Dark comedy is oxygen in a book like this.)
