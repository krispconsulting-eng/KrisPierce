# 05 · LinkedIn suite — four-post September arc

Kris's voice: professional, personal, no hype. One post a week through
September. Post 1 must be finalised before 1 September; Posts 2–4 can take
live numbers during the month. Each post ends with one link.

---

## Post 1 — Campaign open (1 September)

> Today, several hundred people started walking across Australia. Most of
> them will never leave their own suburb.
>
> The **Walk for SCN2A Australia** is open. Through September, our community
> — families, schools, workplaces, clinicians, friends — is covering
> 4,000 km between us: the width of the country, east coast to west coast.
> Everyone walks wherever they are and logs their kilometres, and one marker
> moves west across the map until we've crossed.
>
> Why: SCN2A is one of the more common genetic causes of epilepsy and
> developmental delay. The families living with it need research, peer
> support, and clinicians and schools who understand the condition. That is
> exactly what every dollar of this campaign funds. SCN2A Australia is
> family-led; donations of $2 or more are tax deductible.
>
> You can register solo, enter a workplace team, sponsor a walker per
> kilometre, or donate outright. Logging a walk takes seconds.
>
> 4,000 km is a long way. Together it is four weeks.
>
> 👉 https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk
>
> #WalkForSCN2A #SCN2A #RareDisease #Epilepsy
