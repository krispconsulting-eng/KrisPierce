# 02 · Team Captain Kit

Send this to every founding family and team captain the day they register.

## Welcome, Captain 💜

Thanks for leading a team in the **Walk for SCN2A Australia**. This one page
has everything you need: what the Walk is, how to get your team signed up,
three posts you can share as-is, and a target that works.

### The Walk in two sentences

This September our community walks **4,000 km — the width of Australia, east
coast to west coast — as one virtual team.** Everyone logs their own
kilometres wherever they live, the marker moves west across the map, and
every dollar raised funds SCN2A research and family support.

### Your job as captain (about an hour, total)

1. **Sign up your team** at https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk —
   choose "Start a team", name it, done.
2. **Invite 5–10 people** personally. Direct messages beat broadcast posts
   every time. Family, school parents, workmates, your gym group.
3. **Share one post a week** (three are written for you below).
4. **Log your own kilometres** — teams follow the captain's lead. Logging
   takes seconds: read the distance off your watch, phone or Strava and type
   it in on the site.

### A target that works

- **Team distance:** 10 km per team member per week is the sweet spot —
  ordinary daily walking, no training plan. A team of 8 pledging 10 km each
  per week contributes **320 km** to the crossing in September.
- **Team fundraising:** aim for **$[TEAM-TARGET]** (a good default: $50 per
  member). Sponsorship per kilometre works brilliantly — ask sponsors to give
  it up front as a donation against your pledge, so nobody has to chase
  pledges in October.

Set a target your team will beat, not one that stalls. You can always raise it.

### What to say when someone asks "why?"

> SCN2A is one of the more common genetic causes of epilepsy and
> developmental delay. SCN2A Australia is family-led — the money goes to
> research, peer support for newly diagnosed families, and education for
> clinicians and schools. Donations of $2 or more are tax deductible and the
> receipt is emailed straight away.
