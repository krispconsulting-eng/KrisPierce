# 08 · Email suite

Seven emails covering the full arc: list → walker → donor → thank-you.
Short by design; every email has one job and one button.

---

## §1 Registration open (to the full list / notify-me signups)

**Subject:** Registration is open: Walk for SCN2A Australia
**Preview:** 4,000 km, one community, this September. Claim your place.

> Hi [FIRST-NAME],
>
> It's open. This September, the SCN2A community is walking the width of
> Australia — 4,000 km, east coast to west coast — as one virtual team, and
> registration just went live.
>
> You walk wherever you are, log your kilometres in seconds, and watch one
> shared marker move west across the map. Solo or in a team; 20 km or
> 200 km; walking, wheeling, or pushing a pram — every kilometre counts.
>
> Every dollar raised funds SCN2A research, peer support for families, and
> education for clinicians and schools.
>
> **[Join the Walk →](https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk)**
>
> Teams fill fastest when a captain starts them — if that's you, reply to
> this email and we'll send you the Captain Kit.
>
> See you on the road,
> Kris — SCN2A Australia

## §2 Walker welcome (triggered on registration)

**Subject:** You're in. Here's how the Walk works.
**Preview:** Your pledge, your logging, your team — two minutes to read.

> Hi [FIRST-NAME],
>
> Welcome to the Walk for SCN2A Australia — you're one of [WALKERS] people
> now crossing the country together. Three things worth knowing:
>
> **1. Your kilometres count from 1 September.** Anything you log between
> 1 and 30 September moves our marker west. Ordinary daily walking adds up
> to 60–120 km a month without changing a thing.
>
> **2. Logging takes seconds.** Read the distance from your watch, phone or
> Strava, and type it in on your fundraising page. Totals update instantly.
>
> **3. Sponsors make your steps worth more.** Share your page and ask
> friends to back you — per kilometre or a flat gift. Donations of $2+ are
> tax deductible and receipts are emailed automatically.
>
> **[Go to my fundraising page →](https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/account)**
>
> Weekly awards run all month — Hero, Superhero and Photo of the Week — and
> there are end-of-Walk prizes for the biggest team and the furthest team.
>
> Glad you're with us. 💜
> Kris — SCN2A Australia

## §3 Pre-start briefing (~28 August, to registered walkers)

**Subject:** The Walk starts Tuesday — 60-second checklist
**Preview:** How to log, when leaderboards reset, and your Day 1 job.

> Hi [FIRST-NAME],
>
> Four days until the Walk begins. Your 60-second checklist:
>
> ✅ **Bookmark your page** — logging lives here:
> https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/account
> ✅ **Know your source** — watch, phone or Strava; you'll just copy the
> number across. (Automatic Strava sync is on the roadmap — for now it's
> type-it-in, and it genuinely takes seconds.)
> ✅ **Share your page once before Tuesday** — pre-start sponsors are the
> best motivation on Day 1.
> ✅ **Day 1 job:** walk something — anything — and log it. Then post your
> start-line photo with #WalkForSCN2A. First Photos of the Week get crowned
> Monday.
>
> Leaderboards reset every Monday. Distance posts at every 1,000 km. The
> west coast at 4,000.
>
> See you Tuesday. 👟
> Kris — SCN2A Australia
