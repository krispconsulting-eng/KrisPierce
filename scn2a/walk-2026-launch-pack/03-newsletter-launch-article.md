### This September, we walk the width of Australia

Some distances feel impossible from where you're standing. Four thousand
kilometres — the width of Australia, east coast to west coast — is one of
them. So is the road from a new SCN2A diagnosis to answers.

Neither is impossible together. That's the point of the **Walk for SCN2A
Australia**.

From **1 to 30 September**, our whole community — families, friends, schools,
workplaces, clinicians — will walk 4,000 km between us, one logged kilometre
at a time. It's a virtual event: you walk wherever you are, on your street or
your treadmill or the school oval, and every kilometre you log moves a single
marker west across the map of Australia. When the marker reaches the west
coast, we've crossed the country. All of us.

**Taking part is deliberately simple:**

1. **Sign up** — register solo, or start a team for your family, school,
   workplace or supporters.
2. **Pledge your kilometres** — commit to what you can. 20 km or 200 km;
   every kilometre moves the marker.
3. **Walk and log it** — read your distance from your watch, phone or Strava
   and log it on the site in seconds.
4. **Cross Australia together** — watch the community total climb and the
   marker move west.

There'll be plenty to play for along the way: a **Hero of the Week**,
**Superhero of the Week** and **Photo of the Week** crowned every week,
leaderboards that reset each Monday, distance posts to celebrate at every
1,000 km, and end-of-Walk prizes for the **biggest team** and the **team
that walks the furthest**.

**Where the money goes.** Every dollar raised funds SCN2A research, peer
support and navigation for families, and education for the clinicians,
schools and services who care for our kids. SCN2A Australia is family-led
and keeps overheads low — donations of $2 or more are tax deductible, and
your receipt is emailed automatically.

**Can't walk this September?** You can sponsor a walker or a team — per
kilometre or a flat amount — or simply donate to the campaign. A straight
donation lifts the fundraising total (the map only moves on kilometres, so
we count those separately and honestly).

**[Donate →](https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/donate)**

4,000 km is a long way. Together it is four weeks. See you on the road. 💜
