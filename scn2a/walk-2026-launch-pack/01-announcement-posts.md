## Beat 2 — Formal launch (registration open)

### Facebook (community pages and groups — warm)

> It's on. 🎉 Registration for the Walk for SCN2A Australia is officially open.
>
> **The challenge:** together, we walk 4,000 km in September — the width of
> Australia, east coast to west coast. A virtual event, so you can take part
> from your own street, wherever you are.
>
> **How it works:**
> 1️⃣ Sign up — solo, or start a team for your family, school or workplace
> 2️⃣ Pledge your kilometres — 20 km or 200 km, whatever is yours to give
> 3️⃣ Walk and log it — straight from your watch, phone or Strava, takes seconds
> 4️⃣ Cross Australia together — every kilometre moves our marker west
>
> There are weekly awards (Hero of the Week, Superhero of the Week, Photo of
> the Week), leaderboards, and end-of-Walk prizes for the biggest team and the
> team that walks the furthest.
>
> Every dollar raised funds SCN2A research, peer support for families, and
> education for the clinicians and schools who care for our kids.
>
> 👉 Join the Walk: https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk
>
> Walk, wheel, push a pram or a chair — every kilometre counts. See you on
> the road. 💜
>
> #WalkForSCN2A #SCN2A #RareDisease
