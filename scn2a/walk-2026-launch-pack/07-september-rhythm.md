# 07 · September rhythm — repeatable in-campaign templates

The map is the content engine: two steady beats a week (Monday total,
Thursday spotlight), plus event-driven posts when milestones fall. Fill the
brackets from the /walk live counters and the fundraising admin; each takes
under five minutes.

**Weekly cadence**

| Day | Beat | Template |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | The marker moved — weekly total + leaderboard reset | §1 |
| Thursday | Spotlight — Hero / Superhero / Photo of the Week | §2 |
| As they fall | Distance posts (1,000 / 2,000 / 3,000 km), halfway day | §3–4 |
| ~13–15 Sep | Mid-month lift: Double-KM Weekend | §5 |
| 27–30 Sep | Final 72 hours sequence | §6 |
| 30 Sep / 1 Oct | The finish + thank-you | §7 |

---

## §1 Monday total ("the marker moved")

> Monday map check. 🗺️
>
> Last week you walked **[KM-WEEK] km** — the marker is now **[KM-TOTAL] km**
> from the east coast, [PERCENT]% of the way across Australia. [ONE
> CONCRETE FLOURISH — e.g. "That's past [TOWN/LANDMARK], for those following
> along at home." or "Biggest single week so far."]
>
> Leaderboards just reset — clean slate, new Hero to crown on Thursday.
> Plenty of road left if you want in: your first walk can count today.
>
> 👉 https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk
> #WalkForSCN2A

**Graphic:** progress/map frame with km + % updated.

## §2 Thursday spotlight — weekly awards

Rotate the three awards; with permission, use the person's photo and first
name. Keep it about them, not the campaign.

**Hero of the Week**

> This week's Hero of the Week: **[NAME]**. 🏅
>
> [ONE OR TWO SENTENCES OF THE ACTUAL STORY — e.g. "[NAME] pledged 40 km and
> quietly logged 68, most of it on the school run." Specific beats
> superlative.]
>
> [KM] km for the crossing. Take a bow, [NAME]. 👏
>
> Who's next? Leaderboards run Monday to Sunday — every logged walk is in
> the running. #WalkForSCN2A

**Superhero of the Week**

> Superhero of the Week goes to **[NAME]** — [THE REASON: the against-the-
> odds effort, the team rallied, the wheelchair kilometres, the 2am hallway
> laps that got logged anyway]. 🦸
>
> This award exists because effort doesn't always show up as distance. This
> week it showed up as [THING]. We see you, [NAME]. 💜
> #WalkForSCN2A

**Photo of the Week**

> Photo of the Week. 📸 Taken by **[NAME]** — [WHERE/WHAT in one line].
>
> Post your walks with #WalkForSCN2A or drop them in the comments to be in
> the running next week.

## §5 Mid-month lift — Double-KM Weekend (~13–15 Sep)

If a sponsor match is confirmed, run **Match Day** instead: same structure,
"every dollar doubled by [SPONSOR]" replaces the km hook.

> This weekend only: **Double-KM Weekend**. ✌️
>
> Every kilometre logged on Saturday and Sunday counts twice toward the
> 4,000 km crossing. The long walk you've been saving up? This is its
> weekend.
>
> Rally your team, pick your route, and log it before Sunday midnight.
> 👉 https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk
> #WalkForSCN2A

**Monday result**

> Double-KM Weekend result: **[KM] km** in two days — our biggest weekend of

>

> The gap: [KM-GAP] km and $[DOLLAR-GAP]. That's genuinely closeable —

>
> Whatever you've got left — the lunchtime loop, the school run, the walk

> And if you've cheered all month without walking: today's donation is the
> final push home. https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/donate

## §7 The finish + thank-you (30 Sep / early Oct)

Post everywhere: Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn. Pair with the thank-you tile.

> We crossed Australia. 🇦🇺💜
>
> **[KM-TOTAL] km** walked in 30 days, by **[WALKERS] walkers** across
> **[TEAMS] teams** — and **$[RAISED]** raised for SCN2A research, family
> peer support, and education for clinicians and schools.
>
> Congratulations to our end-of-Walk prize winners:
> 🏆 Biggest team: **[TEAM-NAME]** ([N] members)
> 🏆 Furthest team: **[TEAM-NAME]** ([KM] km)
>
> Thank you to every walker, sponsor and donor[, to [SPONSOR],] and to the
> global SCN2A community walking with us around the world[, especially the
> German team who inspired this campaign].
>
> Donations stay open until [14 October] if you'd like your name on this:
> https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/donate
>
> What your dollars do next — that's a story we'll tell you very soon.
> #WalkForSCN2A

> **If the marker doesn't reach 4,000 km:** lead with the true number,
> proudly, and don't apologise — "[KM-TOTAL] km in 30 days" is the headline,
> the crossing continues next year. Never frame the community's effort as a
> shortfall.
