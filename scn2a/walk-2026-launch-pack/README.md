## Voice rules (apply to every asset)

- Australian English. Warm, plain, concrete. No hype words, no guilt.
- Lead with the community and the crossing, not the charity's needs.
- Kilometres move the map; dollars fund research. Keep the two trackers
  distinct and say plainly that a straight donation lifts the dollars, not the map.
- Every ask is one action: **Join the Walk** or **Donate**. Never both in the
  same breath as equals — pick the primary CTA per post.
- Inclusive by default: walk, wheel, push a pram or a wheelchair — every
  kilometre counts. Many in our community move differently; the copy always
  leaves the door open.
- Facts you can check: 4,000 km, 1–30 September, $2+ tax deductible. If a
  number isn't from the site or the fundraising admin, it doesn't go in a post.
