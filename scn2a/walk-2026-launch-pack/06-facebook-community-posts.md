# 06 · Facebook community posts

For SCN2A community pages and groups. Warmer and more personal than
LinkedIn — this audience already knows what SCN2A is, so don't re-explain
the condition; invite them in.

---

## Launch post (1 September)

> Day one, friends. The Walk for SCN2A Australia has officially started. 👟💜
>
> Between now and 30 September, all of us together are walking 4,000 km —
> the width of Australia, east coast to west coast. Every kilometre you log
> moves our marker a little further west. Every dollar raised goes to SCN2A
> research, family peer support, and education for the people who care for
> our kids.
>
> And here's the lovely part: your ordinary life already counts. The school
> run. The walk to the letterbox and back, again, because someone forgot
> their hat. Laps of the hallway at 2am. Log them. They all move the map.
>
> Walk, wheel, push a pram or a chair — every kilometre counts, from
> anywhere in the world.
>
> 👉 Join in or cheer us on: https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk
>
> Post your Day 1 photo below — first Photo of the Week gets crowned on
> Monday! 📸
>
> #WalkForSCN2A

## Bonus — group engagement prompts (use between the big posts)

Short, no-link posts that keep the group alive without asking for anything.

- "Show us your walking buddy. 🐕👶 Photo thread — go."
- "Where's the strangest place you've logged kilometres from this month?
  Wrong answers only."
- "Shout out a teammate who kept you moving this week. Tag them. 💜"
- "It's Monday — leaderboards just reset. Clean slate. Who's topping it
  this week?"
