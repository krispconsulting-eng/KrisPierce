## Walk for SCN2A Australia: this September, we cross the country together

**This September, our community is walking the width of Australia — 4,000 km,
east coast to west coast — as one virtual team. Registration is open now.**

The **Walk for SCN2A Australia** runs from **1 to 30 September 2026**. It's a
virtual event, which means there is no start line to travel to: you walk
wherever you are — your street, your local park, a treadmill, the school
oval — and log your kilometres on the campaign site. Every kilometre logged
by every walker moves a single marker west across the map of Australia.
When it reaches the west coast, we've crossed the country together.

### How it works

1. **Sign up.** Register solo, or create a team for your family, school,
   workplace or supporters.
2. **Pledge your kilometres.** Commit to what you can — 20 km or 200 km.
   Ordinary daily walking adds up to 60–120 km in a month without changing
   a thing.
3. **Walk and log it.** Read your distance from your watch, phone or Strava
   and log it in seconds, from any device.
4. **Cross Australia together.** Every walker's kilometres add to one total:
   4,000 km from east to west.

Along the way there are weekly awards — **Hero of the Week**, **Superhero of
the Week** and **Photo of the Week** — leaderboards that reset every Monday,
distance posts to celebrate at every 1,000 km, and end-of-Walk prizes for
the biggest team and the team that walks the furthest.

### Where the money goes

Every dollar raised through the Walk funds:

- **Research** that moves SCN2A closer to targeted treatment
- **Peer support and navigation** for families, including those newly diagnosed
- **Education** for the clinicians, schools and services who care for our kids

SCN2A Australia Ltd is a family-led registered charity (ACNC) with deductible
gift recipient status. Donations of $2 or more are tax deductible, and your
receipt is emailed automatically. ABN 70 665 788 161.
