# 09 · Sponsor + prize outreach

Two asks, two sizes: a **campaign sponsor** (the $5–10k conversation, per-km
match angle) and a **prize donor** (small, easy yes — the site already
invites it). Both are email-first, written from Kris. Personalise the
opening line for every recipient; delete anything that doesn't fit.

---

## §1 Campaign sponsor pitch

**Subject:** Walking the width of Australia for SCN2A — a partnership for September

> Hi [NAME],
>
> [PERSONAL LINE — why this company, why now: local connection, staff
> connection to the community, values fit.]
>
> This September, SCN2A Australia is running the **Walk for SCN2A
> Australia**: our whole community — families, schools, workplaces — walking
> 4,000 km between us, the width of the country, as one virtual team. Every
> walker logs their kilometres and a single marker moves west across the
> map. It runs 1–30 September, and every dollar funds SCN2A research, peer
> support for newly diagnosed families, and education for clinicians and
> schools.
>
> I'm inviting [COMPANY] to be the campaign's sponsor. What that looks like:
>
> - **Per-kilometre match.** "[COMPANY] backs every kilometre" — you match
>   community fundraising up to a cap you set, or contribute per 1,000 km
>   distance post reached. Matches visibly accelerate giving, and the daily
>   map gives your support a month of momentum, not one mention.
> - **Acknowledgement all month.** Your support recognised on the campaign
>   site, in the launch newsletter and thank-you to the full community, and
>   in the weekly progress posts across our channels through September.
> - **A team of your own.** Your staff register free as Team [COMPANY] —
>   a ready-made, feel-good workplace wellbeing month with a leaderboard.
>
> SCN2A Australia Ltd is a family-led registered charity (ACNC, DGR status,
> ABN 70 665 788 161), so sponsorship can be structured as a deductible
> donation, and we keep overheads low — your support lands where we say it
> does.
>
> Could we take 20 minutes in the next fortnight? I can share the campaign
> plan and the numbers, and we can shape something that fits [COMPANY].
>
> Warm regards,
> Kris Pierce
> SCN2A Australia · https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk

**Follow-up nudge (one week later, if no reply):**

> Hi [NAME] — floating this back up. The Walk launches [DATE] and I'd love
> [COMPANY]'s name on it from day one. Even a modest per-km match changes
> the shape of the month. Happy to send the one-pager if easier. — Kris

## §2 Prize donor ask (businesses — the easy yes)

At the end of the Walk we award prizes for the **biggest team** and the
**team that walks the furthest**, plus weekly Hero / Superhero / Photo
awards. Local businesses can donate small prizes: vouchers, hampers, gear.

**Subject:** Would [BUSINESS] donate a prize for the Walk for SCN2A?

> Hi [NAME],
>
> [PERSONAL LINE — local/community connection.]
>
> This September, the SCN2A community — families of children with a rare
> genetic epilepsy — is walking 4,000 km together, the width of Australia,
> to fund research and family support. Along the way we crown a Hero,
> Superhero and Photo of the Week, and at the end we award prizes for the
> biggest team and the team that walks the furthest.
>
> Would [BUSINESS] donate a prize? A voucher or a hamper is perfect —
> anything that lets us celebrate a walker properly.
>
> In return we'll acknowledge [BUSINESS] by name when the prize is awarded
> — on our Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn and in the campaign newsletter
> — and, of course, in front of a very grateful community.
>
> If yes, just reply and we'll sort collection. Thank you for considering it.
>
> Kris Pierce
> SCN2A Australia · https://scn2a-fundraising.vercel.app/walk

## §3 Acknowledgement copy (once a sponsor/prize is confirmed)

**Social acknowledgement (drop into any rhythm post):**

> A huge thank-you to **[SPONSOR]**, who are backing every kilometre of the
> Walk — [MATCH TERMS in plain words, e.g. "matching every dollar raised
> this weekend"]. Support like this is why the map keeps moving. 💜

**Prize award acknowledgement:**

> This week's [AWARD] prize comes courtesy of **[BUSINESS]** — thank you
> for backing our walkers! 🎁

**Compliance notes**

- State match terms plainly and honour them exactly as published (cap,
  dates, what triggers the match).
- Acknowledgement is thanks, not advertising: name, logo and link are fine;
  product claims and promotional copy are not.
- Confirm with the sponsor how they want to be named before the first post,
  and get sign-off on the acknowledgement line above.
- Prizes awarded on published criteria (biggest team, furthest team, weekly
  awards) — keep the criteria on the site so winners are verifiable.
