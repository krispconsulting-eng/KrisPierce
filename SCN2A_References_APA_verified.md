**Verification method.** Every reference cited across the manuscript, literature review, and study protocol was checked against an authoritative source — PubMed/CrossRef for indexed journal articles, and the publisher or issuing agency for grey literature and conference abstracts (July 2026).

> **Style note.** APA 7th is applied here as requested. *Research Involvement and Engagement* (the current target journal) uses **numbered Vancouver** style — if that remains the target, these will need converting. The manuscript's own reference section has been set to this APA list.

A closely related, genuinely public study — the **SCN2A Clinical Trial Readiness Study (CTRS)** — is run by the **FamilieSCN2A Foundation** (not Praxis), begun ~2021. **Action needed:** confirm whether your survey/focus-group data (the 75%/65%/80%/70% figures) come from (a) a genuine internal Praxis report SCN2A Australia holds, or (b) the FamilieSCN2A CTRS, and cite accordingly. As an unpublished internal document it can be cited as an organisational record, but the title/author/year must be exact.
