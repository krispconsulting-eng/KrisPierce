Rare disease caregivers carry a load that rarely gets acknowledged, let alone supported. Sponsoring seats is a direct, measurable way to show up for the community you already work with, or want to reach.

Become a sponsor See what you get back

A direct line to real impact, not a diffuse donation

Your sponsorship funds a named number of seats, in a named cohort, for a named person, not a donation into a general fund.

  • Direct, not diffuse. An actual person going through an actual six weeks.
  • Measurable. Every participant takes the Wellness Wheel at week 1 and week 6, so you get real before/after data.
  • Low overhead. You fund seats and receive a report. We run enrollment, delivery, and reporting.

Three ways to sponsor

Named seats: you identify who they're for.
Scholarship pool: seats with no names attached; caregivers apply.
Annual commitment: a set number of seats every year.

Simple pricing, no negotiation needed

$200 per seat, flat, regardless of tier. Tiers reflect commitment level and what you get back, not a different price.

An impact report you can actually use

After every cohort you've sponsored completes, you receive a report built from real participant data, aggregated and anonymised.

  • Wellness Wheel delta: aggregate before/after scores across all 8 dimensions
  • Completion and engagement rate for the seats you funded
  • Opt-in testimonials and quotes from participants
  • Public acknowledgment: your logo and seat count on our Supporters page

We commit to providing reports on our page and acknowledging every sponsor, whether you fund 1 seat or 100.
