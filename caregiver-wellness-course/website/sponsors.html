Rare disease caregivers carry a load that rarely gets acknowledged, let alone supported. Sponsoring seats is a direct, measurable way to show up for the community you already work with, or want to reach.

Direct, not diffuse

Your sponsorship funds a named number of seats, in a named cohort. Not a donation into a general fund, an actual person going through an actual six weeks.

Measurable

Every participant takes the Wellness Wheel at week 1 and week 6. You get aggregate, anonymised before/after data, not just a completion count.

Low overhead for you

You fund seats and receive a report. We run enrollment, delivery, and reporting. No program to manage on your end.

Three ways to sponsor

Choose how your seats are used

Named seats

You identify who the seats are for, for instance, caregivers already in a community or patient group you work with. Each named person gets a free redemption link.

Scholarship pool

You donate seats with no names attached ("10 spots a year"). Caregivers apply directly through our site, and seats go to people who couldn't otherwise afford to join.

Annual commitment

Sponsor a set number of seats every year rather than a one-off round. Gives you a consistent, repeatable impact story to report internally or publicly.

What you get back

An impact report you can actually use

After every cohort you've sponsored completes, you receive a report built from real participant data, aggregated and anonymised.

  • 📊 Wellness Wheel delta: aggregate before/after scores across all 8 dimensions
  • ✅ Completion and engagement rate for the seats you funded
  • 💬 Opt-in testimonials and quotes from participants
  • 🏅 Public acknowledgment: your logo and seat count on our Supporters page
