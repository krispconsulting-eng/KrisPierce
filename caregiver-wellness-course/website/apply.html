Sponsored spots

If cost is what's stopping you, apply for a sponsored spot.

Some organisations sponsor seats specifically for caregivers who couldn't otherwise join. There's no means test and no long form, just a short, honest application. We read every one.

✅

Who this is for

Family or informal caregivers (parents, partners, siblings) of someone with a rare disease, for whom the $97 self-pay price is genuinely a barrier.

📝

What we ask

A little about your situation, in your own words. No documentation, no financial disclosures. We take you at your word.

⏱️

What happens next

We review applications ahead of each new cohort. If approved, you'll get a no-cost redemption link and the same onboarding as everyone else.
